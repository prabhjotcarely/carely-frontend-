Questions? Good.

When does Carely launch? ▾
Carely is launching May 2026 on iOS and Android. Founding members get early access — we'll send you a direct download link the moment it's live.
Does Elara speak languages other than English? ▾
Yes. Elara speaks 10 languages natively: English, French, Hindi, Punjabi, Spanish, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, and Arabic. She responds in your language automatically based on your phone's settings — not a translation, a native response.
What does the AI Caretaker actually do? ▾
Every morning, Elara reviews your last 7 days and sends a short personal message — celebrating streaks, gently checking in on missed doses, or asking how you're feeling. You can also chat with her directly about anything. She never gives medical advice and always refers you to your doctor when it matters.
Is my health data safe? What does the AI see? ▾
Your health data is never sold. Elara only sees your first name, medicine nicknames (never real drug names), and anonymised adherence stats. No diagnoses, no dose amounts, no PII. PIPEDA (Canada) and CCPA (California) compliant.
Can I set this up for a parent or loved one? ▾
Yes — this is exactly what Carely is built for. Set it up on your parent's phone. Share a code with your own account. You'll get a push notification if they miss a dose. You see their schedule and adherence — never their medical notes, dose amounts, or health records. They feel independent. You feel at ease.
