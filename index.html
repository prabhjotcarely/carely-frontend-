Questions? Good.

When does Carely launch? ▾
Carely is launching Q2 2026 on both iOS and Android. Founding members get early access before the public launch — we'll email you a direct TestFlight / Play Store link the moment it's ready.
What does the AI Caretaker actually do? ▾
Every morning, the AI reviews your last 7 days and sends a short personal message — celebrating streaks, gently checking in on missed doses, or asking how you're feeling. You can also chat with it directly. It never gives medical advice.
Is my health data safe? What does the AI see? ▾
Your health data is never sold. The AI only sees your first name, medicine nicknames (not real drug names), and anonymised adherence stats. No diagnosis, no dose amounts, no PII. PIPEDA (Canada) and CCPA (California) compliant.
What happens after my founding year is up? ▾
After 12 months, your plan rolls to the standard $9.99/month. You'll get a 30-day notice email. You can cancel any time — we'll never silently change your rate.
Can I use this for a family member? ▾
Yes. Caregiver Mode links your account to a patient's. You'll receive missed dose alerts via push notification and can see their schedule and adherence — but never dose amounts or doctor notes.
Do I pay now to get the founding price? ▾
No. Sign up with your email today to reserve your spot. You're not charged until launch day when your 7-day free trial starts. The $5/month price is locked from your first payment.
