The Problem We Solve

Non-adherence is a pharmacy problem too.

When patients don't take their medication, they don't refill. They switch pharmacies. They end up in hospital. And the pharmacy that dispensed to them has no visibility into any of it.

💊

50% of patients stop taking chronic medications within the first year

Half of all prescriptions for ongoing conditions — blood pressure, diabetes, cholesterol — are abandoned before the patient should have stopped. That's half your refill revenue walking out the door, silently, every year.

🔄

Non-adherent patients switch pharmacies — they blame the experience, not themselves

When patients feel unsupported after filling a prescription, they drift to whichever pharmacy is closest or cheapest. There's no loyalty because there's no follow-up. Carely creates the follow-up — from you.

📉

You have no visibility once the patient walks out the door

Dispensing is the start of the relationship, not the end. But without any follow-up touchpoint, pharmacies are flying blind on whether patients are taking what they were given — until the next refill, if it comes at all.

How It Works

Recommend once at the counter. Carely does the rest.

No software to install. No workflow changes. Your staff recommend Carely when dispensing — the patient does the setup, and you earn every month they stay subscribed.

1

Recommend Carely at the dispensing counter

When a patient picks up a new chronic medication or a complex regimen, your pharmacist recommends Carely. Hand them a card or show them the QR code. The recommendation takes 20 seconds.

2

Patient downloads and sets up their medication schedule

Elara, Carely's AI assistant, guides the patient through entering their medications and reminder times in plain language. No tech expertise required — if they can send a text, they can use Carely.

3

Carely reminds and checks in at every dose

Every day, at every scheduled dose, the patient gets a gentle reminder and a friendly Elara check-in. Missed doses are flagged. Family members can be notified. Patients who need help get it before it becomes a crisis.

4

Adherent patients refill on time — with you

Patients who take their medication consistently run out on schedule and come back to refill. You earn 20% commission on their Carely subscription every month — and keep a loyal customer who trusts your pharmacy.
