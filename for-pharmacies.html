The Problem We Solve

Non-adherence is a pharmacy problem too.

You spend time counselling every patient who walks out with a new prescription. But once the door closes, you have no visibility into what happens next.

💊

50% of patients stop taking medications within the first year

Despite your best counselling at the counter, half of chronic medication patients will miss doses, lapse, or discontinue entirely — often within weeks of starting.

💸

Non-adherence costs the healthcare system $300B per year

It also costs your pharmacy: lapsed patients don't refill prescriptions, and poor outcomes reflect poorly on the care you provide — even when it isn't your fault.

🚪

You have no visibility once they walk out the door

After the initial fill, you have no way to know if a patient is adhering, struggling, or has quietly stopped. There's no loop back to you — until a readmission or refill lapse signals a problem.

How It Works

Simple to recommend. Powerful for your patients.

There's nothing to install, no system integration, and no change to your workflow. You simply recommend Carely — we do the rest.

1

You recommend Carely at the counter

When a patient picks up a new long-term prescription, mention Carely. Hand them a card or show them the app. It takes 20 seconds.

2

Patients set up reminders and connect your pharmacy

They download Carely, add their medications, and link your pharmacy. The AI caretaker Elara guides them through setup in under 5 minutes.

3

You receive weekly adherence summaries

Every week, Carely emails your pharmacy a formatted adherence summary for consenting patients — who's on track, who's struggling, and who hasn't taken a dose in days.

What Your Patients Get

More than just a reminder. A real support system.

Carely is built for real patients with real lives — people who forget, get busy, and need encouragement, not judgment.

⏰

Smart medication reminders

Gentle, customisable reminders at every scheduled dose time. Patients can snooze, confirm, or mark as taken — all in one tap.

🤖

Elara AI caretaker check-ins
