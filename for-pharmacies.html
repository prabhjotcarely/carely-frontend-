The Problem We Solve

Prescriptions dispensed
aren't prescriptions taken.

The moment a patient walks out of your pharmacy, you lose visibility. Most don't refill on time. Many don't refill at all.

💉

Prescriptions abandoned at refill

38% of chronic medication prescriptions are never refilled after the first dispense. Patients forget, run out, or let the prescription lapse — and you have no way to know until they don't show up.

⌛

Patients forget to call ahead

Most refill pickups are unplanned. Patients arrive when they're already out — or don't arrive at all. Without reminders, forgetfulness costs your pharmacy dispensing revenue every single month.

📊

No visibility into adherence

You dispense, the patient leaves, and you're flying blind. There's no signal about whether they're taking their medication — until the next refill arrives, or it doesn't.

How It Works

Three steps from dispense
to refill request.

No integration. No IT setup. Carely runs on the patient's phone and sends refill signals straight to your pharmacy when supply runs low.

1

Patient uses Carely daily

Elara, Carely's AI, guides the patient through setting up their medication schedule. Every day, at every dose, Carely checks in and tracks how many tablets remain — estimating supply from the dispense date and adherence record.

2

Low-supply alert fires 7 days out

When supply drops below the threshold — default 7 days — Carely triggers a low-supply alert. The patient sees a prominent card with their pharmacy's name, a one-tap refill request, and pickup timing guidance.

3

Pharmacy notified before the patient runs out

When the patient taps "Request refill," your pharmacy receives a structured email with the patient name, medication, and estimated pick-up date — giving you 7 days to prepare. No missed refills. No wasted stock.

Features for Pharmacy Partners

Everything you need to stop losing prescriptions to forgetfulness.

Built for the pharmacy workflow — zero disruption, maximum signal.

Refill request alerts

Receive a structured email the moment a patient requests a refill through Carely — with medication name, quantity, and estimated pick-up date. No more last-minute rushes or out-of-stock surprises.

Patient adherence data

With patient consent, view adherence trends for your patients in the pharmacy partner portal. Identify who is at risk of abandoning their prescription before they do, and reach out proactively.
