The Problem We Solve

What happens between appointments is invisible to you.

You make evidence-based treatment decisions based on what patients tell you. But what they tell you doesn't always match what's actually happening at home.

📋

Patients report 80% adherence — the real number is 50%

Studies consistently show that patients overreport adherence in clinical settings. They're not lying to you — they're embarrassed, or genuinely don't know how many doses they've missed.

🏥

Non-adherent patients return more often and get worse outcomes

Uncontrolled conditions from missed medication lead to more frequent visits, more acute episodes, and higher resource use — for both your practice and the healthcare system.

👁️

You have no way to know what's happening between visits

Between appointments — which can be weeks or months apart — you're operating blind. Carely closes that gap and gives you real data, not just patient recall.

How It Works

Zero workflow change. Full patient visibility.

Carely doesn't ask you to learn new software or integrate with your EMR. Everything arrives in your inbox — ready to read in 60 seconds.

1

Patient connects your name and clinic inside Carely

When a patient sets up Carely, they enter your name and clinic email. From that point, you're linked to their adherence data — with their full consent.

2

You receive a formatted weekly adherence report

Every Monday, a clean one-page PDF summary lands in your inbox. It shows each patient's medication name, dose times, adherence rate, and any concerning patterns.

3

Patients can request appointments directly through the app

If a patient needs to see you, they can request an appointment inside Carely. The request arrives in your inbox — no phone tag, no receptionist bottleneck.

What Your Patients Get

Support between visits — without extra burden on you.

Carely acts as a quiet, always-on clinical support layer — checking in on patients, reminding them of doses, and keeping you informed.

📄

Weekly PDF adherence reports

A one-page summary of each patient's medication adherence lands in your inbox weekly. Glance at it in 60 seconds — and know exactly who needs follow-up.

📅

Appointment request emails

Patients can initiate appointment requests from inside the app. Formatted, timestamped, and sent straight to your inbox — no calls required.

🚨

Missed dose alerts

If a patient misses multiple consecutive doses, you receive an alert — giving you the chance to follow up proactively before an acute event occurs.

🤖

Elara AI monitors between visits

Carely's AI companion Elara checks in daily with patients — asking how they're feeling and flagging symptoms or concerns. You receive a summary, not noise.

💻

No software to install — ever

Everything arrives as an email. No login, no portal, no new system. Works with whatever inbox you already use — Gmail, Outlook, or your clinic's email provider.
