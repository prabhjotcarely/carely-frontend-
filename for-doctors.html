The Problem

You prescribe it. But do they take it?

Non-adherence is the silent epidemic behind most chronic disease complications. Right now, you have no visibility between appointments.

📸

No between-visit data

You see patients every 3–6 months. Between visits, you have zero information about whether prescriptions are actually being followed.

⚠

Missed doses go unreported

Patients often don’t mention non-adherence. They forget, or feel embarrassed. By the time you find out, complications may have already started.

📊

No time for manual follow-up

Your team doesn’t have bandwidth to call every patient about their daily meds. You need passive intelligence, not more admin work.

How DrBridge Works

Three steps. Zero workflow change.

Carely plugs into your existing practice without touching your EMR or adding any charting burden.

1

You apply & get your DrBridge link

We create a unique shareable link for your practice. Give it to patients during any appointment, or send it by text — takes 30 seconds.

2

Elara tracks their daily doses

Your patients download Carely and Elara, our AI caretaker, checks in with them every morning. Each dose is logged, each mood noted, every side effect flagged.

3

You get weekly adherence reports

Every Sunday, a clean summary lands in your inbox: adherence percentage, missed doses, flagged concerns, and patients requesting a follow-up.
