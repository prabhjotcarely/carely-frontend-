Our Story

Built for the families
who never stop worrying

Carely started with a simple, frustrating question: why is it so hard to make sure your aging parent took their medication today?

Where it started

Prabh — a registered healthcare worker from BC, Canada — watched the same scene play out across families she worked with. An aging parent. A complicated medication schedule. Adult children calling daily to ask "did you take it?" Growing guilt when they couldn't call. Growing anxiety when there was no answer.

The tools available were either basic alarm apps that seniors ignored or clinical software designed for hospitals, not families. Nothing in between that felt human, warm, and actually worked.

"I wanted something that would talk to my mum like I would — not just ping her. Something that remembered if she'd been dizzy lately, and actually told the family."

— Prabh, Co-founder & COO

So in early 2026, Prabh and TJ started building Carely — not as a tech product, but as a family tool. One that could check in every morning, log doses, alert the family when something was missed, and keep the doctor informed without anyone having to remember to do it manually.

The result is Elara — Carely's AI caretaker. She's not a chatbot. She's the steady, reliable presence that makes sure nothing slips through the cracks — so families can stop worrying and start living.

What we believe

💊

Missed doses are a real safety risk

Half of chronic disease patients don't take medications as prescribed. The consequences — hospitalizations, complications, preventable deaths — are entirely avoidable with the right support.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family should be in the loop, not in the dark

Caregivers — whether down the street or across the country — deserve real-time visibility without becoming full-time monitors. Peace of mind shouldn't require constant checking.

🤖

AI should feel like care, not surveillance

Elara checks in with warmth and context — not cold alerts. She remembers previous conversations, notices patterns, and responds like someone who genuinely cares about the person she's talking to.

🔒

Health data is sacred

We are PIPEDA and CCPA compliant. We never sell health data. We never use it for advertising. Your family's health information belongs to your family — full stop.
