Common Questions

Does Carely connect to blood pressure monitors or glucometers via Bluetooth?
Not currently. Your parent reads the number from their device and types it into Carely. Manual entry takes 10 seconds and works with any brand of monitor or glucometer — no compatibility issues.
What counts as an out-of-range blood pressure reading?
Carely uses standard clinical thresholds. High blood pressure alert triggers above 140/90 (hypertension stage 2). Low blood pressure alert triggers below 90/60. These are guidelines — always follow your parent's doctor's advice.
What blood sugar levels trigger an alert?
Fasting glucose above 130 mg/dL or below 70 mg/dL triggers a family alert. Post-meal readings above 180 mg/dL trigger an alert. These are standard diabetic monitoring thresholds.
Does Carely give medical advice based on the readings?
Never. Carely flags readings and alerts family. Any alert includes a recommendation to consult a doctor. Carely is a monitoring tool, not a diagnostic tool.
Can multiple family members see the vitals readings?
Yes. All connected caregivers see the same real-time vitals dashboard. Add family members from the app — no limit on connections.
