How it works

1. Log your reading in under 15 seconds

Take your reading from your glucometer (any brand — no Bluetooth required). Open Carely, tap "Log vitals", enter the number. Done. Elara confirms it and asks how you're feeling.

2. Elara prompts you at the right time

Set a schedule for fasting readings (morning), post-meal readings (2 hours after eating), or any other time. Elara sends a personalised reminder — not a generic alarm. You're more likely to actually log it.

3. Family gets alerted if something's off

Out-of-range reading? Every connected family member or caregiver gets an instant push notification with the exact reading. They can call or check in immediately.

4. Doctor gets the weekly report automatically

Carely compiles a full glucose log with readings, timestamps, and any medication or supplement taken around those readings. Sent to the connected doctor every week — automatically.
