Common Questions

Can I track supplements that aren't in any drug database?
Yes. Carely uses completely free-text supplement names. Type anything — Lion's Mane, Berberine, NAC, GABA, Alpha-GPC, your protein powder brand name — it all works. No database, no restrictions.
Does Carely support complex supplement stacks?
Yes. Add unlimited supplements, each with their own schedule, dose amount, and dose type. Morning vitamins at 7am, pre-workout at 5pm, magnesium at 10pm — all separate, all tracked, all reminded.
Can I track supplements AND medications in one app?
Yes. Carely makes no distinction between medications and supplements — they live in the same schedule, the same log, and the same dashboard. One app for everything.
Does it work for family members who take supplements too?
Yes. The family caregiver mode lets you connect to a parent or partner's account. You see their schedule, get alerts if they miss a dose, and can add supplements to their routine remotely.
Is Carely available in Canada?
Yes. Carely is built for both the USA and Canada. The founding team is based in BC, Canada. All pricing available in USD and CAD.
