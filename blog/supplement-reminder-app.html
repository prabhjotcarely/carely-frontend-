Common Questions

Can I add supplements that aren't in a drug database?
Yes. Carely uses completely free-text medicine names. Type any supplement name — Lion's Mane, Berberine, NAC, whatever — and it works. There is no validation against a drug database.
Does the app support gummy vitamins?
Yes. Gummies is one of the 12 supported dose types alongside pill, capsule, liquid, powder, drops, and more. Set the dose count and schedule the same way as any medication.
Can I track supplements AND prescriptions in the same app?
Yes. Carely has no distinction between medications and supplements — they're all items in the same schedule. One app, one view, one family dashboard.
Will I know if my parent forgot their vitamins?
Yes. If your parent doesn't confirm a supplement dose by the scheduled time, Carely sends a push notification to every connected family member instantly.
Is Carely available in Canada?
Yes. Carely is built for both the USA and Canada. The founding team is based in BC, Canada. All pricing available in USD and CAD.
