Common Questions

I take Metformin twice a day with meals. Can Carely handle meal-timed reminders?
Yes. Add Metformin as two separate schedule entries — with breakfast and with dinner. Each has its own reminder time and its own adherence log. You can see your morning Metformin adherence rate vs. your evening rate independently.
I use both basal and bolus insulin. Can Carely track both?
Yes. Add them as separate medications with their own schedules — Lantus once at bedtime, Humalog three times daily before meals. Each is tracked independently. The log preserves every dose with timestamp, dose type (injection), and confirmation status.
Does Carely connect to my CGM (Continuous Glucose Monitor)?
Carely does not currently integrate with CGM devices directly. Blood sugar is manually logged in the app. CGM integration is on the product roadmap. For now, Carely is most valuable for medication adherence, manual glucose logging, and the family alert + doctor report layer.
Can my parent with diabetes use Carely if I set it up for them?
Yes. You can set up Carely on your parent's phone and manage everything remotely as a connected caregiver. They only need to respond to Elara's reminders. You see their full medication log, glucose readings, and receive alerts immediately if anything is missed.
