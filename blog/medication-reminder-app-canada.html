Why Canadian families need a different kind of app

Most medication reminder apps are built by American companies for American users. They don't account for Canadian healthcare workflows, provincial pharmacy systems, or the reality of families spread across BC, Ontario, Alberta, and Quebec — often hours apart from their elderly loved ones.
