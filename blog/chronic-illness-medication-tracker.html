Common Questions

I take 8 medications across 3 conditions. Can Carely handle this?
Yes. Carely supports unlimited medications per account. Each has its own name, schedule, dose type, and independent adherence tracking. You can add all 8 medications, set their individual schedules, and Carely tracks every dose across all of them simultaneously.
Some of my medications must be taken with food and others without. Can I track this?
Yes. Each medication has a notes field where you can add timing instructions — "30 min before food," "with breakfast," "at bedtime." Elara sends the reminder at the scheduled time and you can include that context in the reminder message.
Can I connect multiple specialists — cardiologist, endocrinologist, and GP?
Yes. Carely supports multiple doctor connections. Add each specialist. Each connected doctor receives the same weekly adherence and vitals report automatically. You don't have to send different reports to different providers.
Does Carely track vitals like blood pressure and blood sugar too?
Yes. Blood pressure (systolic/diastolic/pulse), blood sugar (glucose), and weight are all tracked in the same app. Family gets alerts if readings fall outside normal ranges. These are included at no extra cost — not a separate subscription.
Is Carely available in Canada?
Yes. Built for both USA and Canada. The founding team is based in BC, Canada.
