What Elara actually does

Elara is not a chatbot. She is not a reminder bell. She is an AI caretaker with memory, personality, and genuine care built in — the kind of daily presence that tells your parent someone is looking out for them, even when you can't be there.

🌅
Daily Morning Check-In
Every morning Elara sends your parent a warm, personalised message. Asks how they're feeling. Confirms their first medications of the day.
💊
Medication & Supplement Tracking
Every scheduled medication and supplement gets a reminder from Elara. She tracks whether it was taken, snoozed, or missed — and logs everything.
📊
Vitals Monitoring
Blood pressure. Blood sugar. Weight. Elara prompts your parent to log their readings and flags anything outside normal range to your family.
🔔
Family Alerts
If your parent misses a dose or doesn't respond to a check-in, Elara alerts every connected family member instantly. You know before it becomes a crisis.
🧠
Memory & Context
Elara remembers your parent's name, conditions, history, and preferences. Every conversation is personal — not a generic template.
🩺
Doctor Reports
Elara compiles weekly adherence reports and sends them to your parent's doctor automatically. The care team stays informed without extra work.

Why Elara exists

Carely was built by Prabh, a healthcare worker who watched patients miss critical medications every week at work — and then came home to worry about her own parents doing the same thing from three hours away.

She and her husband TJ built Elara because no app existed that felt warm, personal, and genuinely caring. Every medication reminder app they tried felt clinical, cold, and generic. Elara is the opposite. She's the AI caretaker they wished existed when they first started worrying.

Common Questions About Elara

Does Elara give medical advice?
Never. Elara monitors adherence and emotional wellbeing only. Any concern she flags always includes a clear recommendation to consult a doctor. She is a caretaker, not a clinician.
How does Elara talk to my parent?
Through the Carely app on their phone. Elara sends warm, personalised in-app messages — not robotic notifications. Your parent replies by tapping or typing. It feels like texting a caring friend.
Does Elara work for supplements, not just medications?
Yes. Elara tracks any supplement — Vitamin D, Omega-3, Magnesium, Ashwagandha, Zinc, anything. Just type the name and set the schedule. Elara handles the rest.
What vitals can Elara track?
Blood pressure, blood sugar (glucose), and weight. More vitals coming soon. Elara prompts your parent to log readings at scheduled times and alerts your family if readings are outside normal range.
Is Elara available in Canada?
Yes. Carely and Elara are built for both the USA and Canada. The founding team is based in BC, Canada. All pricing is available in USD and CAD.
What AI powers Elara?
Elara runs on GPT-4o with persistent memory via Redis. She remembers each user's medication history, conditions, and conversation context for up to 7 days — giving every interaction genuine continuity.
