Common Questions

Does Carely's AI give medical advice?
No — Carely's AI never gives medical advice. It monitors adherence, checks on wellbeing, and alerts family members. Any health concerns flagged by the AI always include a recommendation to speak with their doctor.
How does Elara talk to my parent?
Through the Carely app on their phone. Elara sends a gentle in-app message after each scheduled medication. Your parent responds by tapping or typing — it's as simple as replying to a text message.
What happens if my parent misses a dose?
Elara tries to reach out once. If there's no response after the check-in window, all connected family members receive an immediate push notification alert. You can then call or check in directly.
Is this a replacement for in-home care?
No — Carely is a complement to in-home care, not a replacement. It fills the gaps between caregiver visits, especially for medication monitoring and daily wellbeing check-ins. For seniors with serious medical needs, in-person care is still essential.
