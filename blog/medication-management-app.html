Common Questions

How is Carely different from a basic medication reminder app?
Most reminder apps are one-directional: they send a notification and stop. Carely closes the loop — the dose must be confirmed, the confirmation is logged, family is alerted if it's not confirmed, the doctor receives the weekly log. It's a complete medication management system, not just a timer.
Can a pharmacist or care coordinator use Carely?
Carely's doctor connection feature allows any healthcare provider to receive weekly adherence reports — including pharmacists and care coordinators. Each connected provider receives the same formatted weekly report automatically.
Does Carely store health data securely?
Yes. Carely is built with PIPEDA (Canada) and CCPA (US) compliance in mind. Health data is encrypted at rest. We never sell health data. Users own their data and can export or delete it at any time.
Is Carely available in Canada?
Yes. Built for both USA and Canada. The founding team is based in BC, Canada.
