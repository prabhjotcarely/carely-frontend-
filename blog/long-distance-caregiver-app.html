Common Questions

My parent isn't very tech-savvy. Will they be able to use Carely?
Yes. Carely was designed with elderly users in mind. Large buttons, simple taps, and Elara's warm conversational messages make it easy. Most seniors get comfortable within 2-3 days. You can set everything up for them remotely in under 10 minutes.
Can I add my brother and sister as caregivers too?
Yes. Carely supports multiple connected caregivers. Add as many family members as you like. Everyone sees the same real-time view and receives the same alerts. No one is left out of the loop.
What happens if my parent doesn't respond to the morning check-in?
Carely sends a reminder. If still no response within the alert window, every connected caregiver receives a push notification. The alert includes the time of the missed check-in so you know exactly what happened.
Does Carely work in both USA and Canada?
Yes. Carely is built for North American families. The founding team is based in BC, Canada. Pricing is available in USD and CAD.
Is my parent's health data private?
Yes. Carely is PIPEDA (Canada) and CCPA (USA) compliant. Health data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell health data. Only the patient and their connected caregivers can see the data.
