No. Carely uses manual entry for vitals. You read your number from your blood pressure monitor or glucometer and type it in. Takes 15 seconds. Works with any brand of device — no Bluetooth pairing, no compatibility issues.
Can my family see my health data?
Only connected caregivers you explicitly add can see your data. You control who has access. Carely is PIPEDA (Canada) and CCPA (USA) compliant. Health data is encrypted at rest and in transit and is never sold.
Can I manage both my own health and a parent's in the same app?
Yes. You can have your own medication and supplement schedule, and separately connect as a caregiver to your parent's account. You see both — your own and theirs — in Carely. One subscription.
