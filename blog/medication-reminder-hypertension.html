Common Questions

I take two BP medications — one in the morning, one at night. Can Carely track both?
Yes. Add each as a separate medication with its own schedule. Amlodipine at 9am, Metoprolol at 9pm — each gets its own reminder, its own confirmation, and its own adherence log. You can see which dose you're more consistent with.
My BP target from my doctor is different from the standard thresholds. Can I customize alerts?
Carely uses standard threshold defaults for alerts. Custom threshold customization is on the roadmap. For now, use the notes field to record your doctor's specific targets and reference them when logging readings.
Can I monitor my parent's blood pressure remotely?
Yes. Set up Carely on your parent's phone. They log BP readings after measuring. You see every reading in the family dashboard in real time. If a reading is above 140/90, you get an instant alert — wherever you are.
Does the blood pressure log go to the doctor automatically?
Yes. The weekly doctor report includes the full BP reading log alongside medication adherence data. The cardiologist or GP receives every reading — not just what was remembered at the appointment.
