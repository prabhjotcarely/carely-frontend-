Common Questions

Why does Carely have a different domain than carely.one?
Carely (carely.fit) and carely.one are completely different products. Carely.fit is built by Lumina Path Inc., a BC Canada company. We focus specifically on elderly medication management with an AI caretaker — a very different product category from general wellness apps.
Can I import my data from another medication reminder app?
Currently Carely doesn't have an automated import tool, but setup takes under 10 minutes. You add each medication and supplement manually — most families with 5-10 items are fully set up in one sitting.
How is Elara different from a regular notification?
Elara is an AI with persistent memory — she knows your parent's name, their medications, their health history. Every message is personalised. She asks how they're feeling. She adapts based on context. A regular notification is a beep. Elara is a relationship.
