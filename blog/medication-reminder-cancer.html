Common Questions

My treatment schedule changes week to week. Can Carely handle that?
Yes. You can update, pause, or modify any medication schedule at any time. Cycle-based oral chemo (e.g., 14 days on / 7 days off) can be managed by pausing and restarting the schedule. The log preserves all historical doses accurately.
Can I use Carely during IV chemotherapy treatment, not just oral?
Carely is most useful for the oral medications taken at home — chemotherapy pills, anti-nausea drugs, steroids, and supplements. IV chemotherapy administered at an infusion centre is managed by the clinical team and doesn't require a home reminder app.
Can I track cancer supplements alongside the medications?
Yes. Many cancer patients take supplements alongside treatment — Vitamin D, Omega-3, mushroom extracts, antioxidants. Carely tracks all of these in the same app with the same schedule and the same log. Note: always check with your oncologist before taking supplements during cancer treatment.
Can a caregiver manage the app on behalf of the patient?
Yes. A family caregiver can set up the patient's schedule, monitor the dashboard, and receive all alerts. The patient only needs to respond to Elara's reminders — the caregiver handles everything else remotely.
