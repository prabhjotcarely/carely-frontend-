Common Questions

I forget to check my phone. Will Carely actually help?
Elara's messages are designed to feel personal enough to stop the scroll. But honestly — if you forget your phone entirely, no app fixes that. What Carely does fix is the "I picked up my phone, saw the notification, and dismissed it without thinking" problem. The personalised message + required response creates a pause that generic alarms don't.
Can I see if I forgot my morning vs. afternoon dose?
Yes. Each scheduled dose is tracked independently with its own adherence rate. If your morning Adderall is at 90% but your noon dose is at 60%, you can see that — and tell your doctor.
Does Carely share data with my doctor automatically?
Yes — if you connect a doctor. Carely sends a weekly adherence report to the connected doctor. If you haven't connected one, the log is still available for you to reference at appointments.
I take Adderall IR twice a day at different times. Can Carely handle that?
Yes. Add Adderall as two separate schedule entries — 9am and 1pm, or whatever your timing is. Each has its own reminder and its own log entry. Carely handles complex multi-dose routines with no issue.
