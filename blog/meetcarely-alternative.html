The difference: coordination vs. care

Most family care apps are about coordination — shared calendars, notes, task lists for caregivers. That's useful. But it doesn't actually help your parent take their medications or feel cared for.

Carely is about direct care. Elara talks to your parent every single day. She's the one in the relationship with them — not you managing a dashboard. That's what makes Carely different from any care coordination tool.

Common Questions

Does Carely replace MeetCarely for care coordination?
If your primary need is medication adherence and daily check-ins for an aging parent, Carely does that better than any coordination app. If you need a full family task management system for multiple caregivers managing visits, appointments, and logistics, you may want both. But most families find Carely handles the highest-priority need — did they take their meds today?
How does Elara "personalise" the reminders?
Elara knows your parent's first name, their specific medications and supplements, their health conditions, and their conversation history. She references these in every message. "Good morning Margaret, time for your Metformin and Vitamin D — how's your blood sugar today?" is very different from a generic push notification.
Can I connect multiple family members?
Yes. Add as many family caregivers as needed. Each one gets the same real-time view and the same instant alerts. One Carely subscription covers the whole family.
Is Carely available in Canada?
Yes. Carely is built for both the USA and Canada. The founding team is based in BC, Canada. Pricing available in USD and CAD.
