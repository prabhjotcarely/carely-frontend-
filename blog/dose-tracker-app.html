Common Questions

What happens if I forget to confirm a dose?
After the alert window passes without a response, Carely marks the dose as missed — automatically. You don't have to manually log a miss. The log stays accurate even when you forget.
Can I track doses at multiple times per day for the same medicine?
Yes. Each time slot is tracked independently. Metformin at 8am and Metformin at 6pm each have their own dose log entry, their own alert, and their own adherence rate. You can see exactly which time slot you're dropping.
How far back does the dose history go?
Carely stores your complete dose history from the day you start — no rolling 30-day limit. Reference any dose from any date since you created your account.
Can I track dose amounts that change over time?
Yes. Update the dose amount for any medicine at any time. The log preserves the previous dose amount for entries before the change, and uses the new amount going forward. Accurate historical record throughout.
Is Carely available in Canada?
Yes. Carely is built for both the USA and Canada. The founding team is based in BC, Canada. Pricing available in USD and CAD.
