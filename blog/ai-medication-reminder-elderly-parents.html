Common Questions

What AI powers Elara?
Elara runs on GPT-4o with persistent memory via Redis. She remembers each user's medication history, conditions, and conversation context for up to 7 days — giving every interaction genuine continuity.
Will my parent actually respond to AI messages?
Most do. Elara's messages feel like receiving a text from a caring friend, not a system notification. Seniors who use Carely report feeling less alone — like someone friendly checks in every day.
Does Elara give medical advice?
Never. Elara monitors adherence and wellbeing only. Any concern she flags always includes a clear recommendation to consult a doctor. She is a caretaker, not a clinician.
How many medications can Elara track?
Unlimited. Add as many medications and supplements as your parent takes. Each gets its own schedule, reminder, and log. Elara handles complex multi-medication routines with different times and frequencies.
