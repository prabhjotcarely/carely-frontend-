Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026  |  Effective: May 27, 2026

Short version: We collect only what's needed to run the app. We never sell your health data. You own your data and can delete it at any time. We comply with PIPEDA (Canada) and CCPA (California).

1. Who We Are

Carely is operated by Lumina Path (Carely), a sole proprietorship registered in British Columbia, Canada. References to "we", "us", or "Carely" in this policy mean Lumina Path (Carely).

Contact: hello@carely.fit

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Sending medication reminders at your scheduled times
  • Generating AI caretaker check-ins via Elara
  • Producing formatted health reports for your doctor (only on your request)
  • Alerting caregivers when you miss a dose (only if you set up caregiver access)
  • Processing your subscription payment via Stripe
  • Sending service emails (welcome, password reset, subscription receipts)
  • Improving the product using anonymised, aggregated analytics

We never use your data to train AI models. We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Carely stores data in the following locations:

  • Primary database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) — hosted on AWS US East (N. Virginia, USA). Supabase holds SOC 2 Type II certification. We do not currently have a Canadian-region option; by creating an account you consent to your data being processed in the United States.
  • Cache / AI memory: Upstash Redis — US region. Elara conversation context, retained for 7 days then automatically deleted.
  • Push notifications: Google Firebase (FCM) — US servers.
  • Email: SendGrid (Twilio) — US servers.
  • Payments: Stripe — PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We never store raw card numbers.

All data transfers from Canada to the US are protected under contractual safeguards with each provider.

5. Your Rights Under PIPEDA (Canada)

  • Right to access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: Delete your account and all associated data. This is available in-app under Settings → Delete My Account, or by emailing us.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.
  • Right to complain: You may file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca.

6. Your Rights Under CCPA (California)

California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact hello@carely.fit.

7. Data Retention

  • Active account data: retained while your account is open.
  • Deleted accounts: all health data purged within 30 days. Billing records retained 7 years (legal requirement).
  • Elara AI memory: auto-deleted after 7 days of inactivity.
  • Dose logs: retained for 2 years to support long-term adherence trends, then anonymised.

8. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3.
  • All data at rest is encrypted by Supabase (AES-256).
  • Health data is never stored in plain text.
  • Access to production data is restricted to authorised administrators only.
  • In the event of a breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

9. Children

Carely is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately.

10. AI Caretaker (Elara)

Elara is powered by OpenAI GPT-4o. Your check-in messages and medication context are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses. OpenAI processes this data under their data processing agreement and does not train models on API data. Elara never provides medical advice — always consult your doctor for medical decisions.

11. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to external resources (e.g., health articles). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 14 days before they take effect. The current version is always available at carely.fit/privacy.

Questions or requests?

Email us at hello@carely.fit — we respond within 5 business days.
