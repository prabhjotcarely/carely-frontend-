How Carely works for caregivers

Set up once. Stay connected always.

Three steps to go from constant worry to quiet confidence. No technical knowledge needed on either side.

1

Your loved one adds Carely

They download the app, add their medications, and Elara — Carely's AI companion — walks them through setup in friendly, plain language. Takes about 5 minutes.

2

They invite you as caregiver

They send you a caregiver invite. You accept with one tap and you're immediately connected to their adherence data — no passwords, no portal, no technical setup.

3

You see their status — no intrusion

Open the app anytime to see whether each medication has been taken. Miss a dose? You get an instant notification so you can reach out — instead of calling constantly to check.
