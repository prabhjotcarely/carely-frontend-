The Problem We Solve

The worry of not knowing — from a distance.

Caring for an aging parent or sick family member from a distance is one of the most emotionally exhausting experiences there is. Carely exists because of that exact feeling.

😔

You lie awake wondering if mum took her evening pill

The quiet anxiety of not knowing is relentless. Did they take it? Did they forget? Is something wrong? The only way to find out is to call — and even then, you're not sure you can trust the answer.

📵

Calling to remind them feels controlling — and they don't always answer

Reminding an adult parent to take their medication is a delicate thing. It can feel intrusive to them, and exhausting to you. And when they don't answer, the worry only gets worse.

📍

You're managing their care from a distance with no visibility

You're making decisions, coordinating with doctors, and worrying constantly — but you have no real-time view of what's happening. Carely gives you that visibility, without making your loved one feel watched.

How It Works

Set up once. Stay connected always.

Carely is designed to be as simple for your loved one as it is powerful for you. No technical skill required on either end.

1

Your loved one sets up Carely on their phone

They download the app, add their medications, and set their reminder times. Carely's AI companion Elara walks them through it — in plain, friendly language. Takes about 5 minutes.

2

They invite you as a caregiver — you accept with one tap

They send you a caregiver invite. You accept, and you're immediately connected to their adherence data. No passwords, no portal — just a simple link.

3

You see real-time adherence and get instant alerts

Open the app anytime to see whether each medication has been taken. If a dose is missed, you get an instant notification — so you can check in if needed, instead of checking in constantly.

What You Both Get

Peace of mind for you. Independence for them.

Carely is designed to empower your loved one — not surveil them. They stay in control. You stay informed. It's a partnership, not a tracker.

📡

Real-time adherence visibility

See a live view of your loved one's medication status — taken, missed, upcoming — from your phone, anytime. No calls needed to find out.

🚨

Instant missed dose alerts

If they miss a critical dose, you get notified immediately. You can choose to call, send a message, or simply note it — without making them feel monitored 24/7.

🤖

Elara AI checks in so they don't feel alone

Elara has a friendly daily conversation with your loved one — asking how they're feeling, offering encouragement, and gently reminding them of doses. It's like a caring presence in their day.

📊

Caregiver dashboard

Your caregiver view shows adherence history, streaks, and any patterns — so you can share accurate information with their doctor at the next appointment.

🧡

No medical knowledge needed

You don't need to know anything about medications to use Carely. Set it up, accept the invite, and you're in. Carely does the clinical thinking — you just stay connected.
