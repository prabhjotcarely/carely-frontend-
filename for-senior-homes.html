The Reality in Senior Living

The gaps that keep administrators up at night

Medication management in senior care is fragmented, reactive, and dangerously opaque. Your staff works hard — but the tools haven't kept up.

⚠️

No real-time visibility

Staff chart after the fact. By the time a missed dose is documented, the window for intervention has closed — and families are already calling.

📞

Family calls consume your team

"Did Mum take her medication today?" — multiplied across 40 residents. Your nurses become call centre agents instead of caregivers.

📋

Compliance reporting is manual

Audit time means hours of paper-chasing. Medication logs are incomplete, inconsistent, and nearly impossible to aggregate across your whole community.

How It Works

From setup to full visibility in 48 hours

No hardware. No complex IT integration. Your residents start using Carely within two days of onboarding.

1

We onboard your community

Our team imports your resident list, medication schedules, and family contacts. White-label branding applied.

2

Residents get the Carely app

Simple, large-text interface. Elara guides each resident through their schedule with friendly reminders — no tech experience needed.

3

Families get live access

Family members join as caregivers. They see adherence in real time and receive automatic alerts — without calling your front desk.

4

Your team gets the dashboard

Care staff see every resident's status at a glance. Missed doses trigger escalation alerts. Reports export in one click for audits.
