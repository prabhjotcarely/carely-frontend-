The Problem We Solve

Three gaps in resident care that cost communities every day.

Senior living communities deliver exceptional care — but medication management between scheduled staff visits is one of the hardest gaps to close.

💊

Residents forget doses between staff visits — especially evenings and weekends

Scheduled medication rounds can't cover every dose for every resident. Self-managed evening and weekend medications are the highest-risk window — and also the hardest to monitor without significantly increasing staff hours.

📞

Family members call constantly to check in — pulling staff away from care

Concerned families call the front desk, nursing stations, and care coordinators to check on medication compliance. These calls consume hours of staff time every week — time that belongs at the bedside.

⚠️

Medication non-compliance leads to hospitalisations and liability

A missed dose of a blood thinner, a skipped insulin dose, or a forgotten antihypertensive can have serious consequences — and raise difficult questions about whether your community caught it in time.

How It Works

Simple to deploy. Immediate impact for staff and families.

Carely requires no integration with your care management system. Residents and their families set it up themselves — your staff simply recommend it.

1

Residents (or families) set up Carely on move-in

During the move-in process, your team recommends Carely. The resident or a family member downloads the app and enters their medications and scheduled times — guided by Elara AI in plain, simple language.

2

Carely reminds and checks in at every dose time

At each scheduled dose, the resident receives a gentle notification. Elara follows up with a friendly check-in to confirm they've taken it — and flags any missed dose immediately.

3

Families are alerted — reducing calls to your staff

When a dose is missed, the resident's designated family caregiver receives an instant notification. They can follow up directly, without calling your staff first — significantly reducing after-hours call volume.

What Your Community Gets

Better outcomes for residents. Less burden on staff.

Carely is a resident-facing tool — but the benefits ripple through your entire community, from staffing to family relations to care quality metrics.

📉

Automated dose reminders reduce staff burden

Self-managed medication reminders mean staff spend less time chasing doses and more time on hands-on care. Carely doesn't replace your care team — it extends their reach.

📵

Family alert system cuts check-in calls significantly

When families know they'll be notified if something goes wrong, they stop calling to check. Communities report a meaningful reduction in unsolicited family calls within weeks of recommending Carely.

🤖

Elara AI provides daily companionship check-ins

Elara has a friendly, warm conversation with residents every day — asking how they're feeling, noting any concerns, and providing companionship between staff visits. Residents feel less alone.

Adherence reports for care coordinators

Care coordinators can receive weekly adherence summaries for residents who consent — giving you early warning of compliance issues before they become clinical events.
