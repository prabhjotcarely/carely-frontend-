The Problem We Solve

What happens between appointments is where chronic disease is won or lost.

GPs and family physicians do exceptional work during the consultation. But once the patient leaves, there's no system keeping them on track — until something goes wrong and they're back in your clinic or in the ED.

💊

Patients with chronic conditions frequently miss doses between appointments

Hypertension, diabetes, high cholesterol, thyroid disorders — all require consistent daily medication. But busy lives, forgetfulness, and poor routine mean many patients are significantly non-adherent between your consultations, without either of you knowing.

📞

Worried family members call reception to check in — overwhelming your admin team

When an elderly patient's family isn't sure if their parent is taking their medication, they call your clinic. Repeat calls for non-clinical check-ins consume reception time and frustrate patients waiting to get through for real appointments.

🏥

Non-adherence leads to avoidable complications and preventable ED visits

A patient who skips their antihypertensive for a week is at real risk. A diabetic who misses insulin doses compounds their long-term complications. These aren't random events — they're predictable consequences of the gap between clinic visits that Carely is designed to close.

How It Works

Recommend at the end of the consultation. Carely takes it from there.

No integration with your EMR. No new clinical workflow. Your GP or nurse recommends Carely at the end of an appointment — the patient sets it up at home, and Elara AI keeps them on track.

1

Recommend Carely at the end of the consultation

After issuing a prescription for a chronic condition, your clinician recommends Carely as a medication adherence tool. A QR code or card is all the patient needs. The recommendation adds under 30 seconds to your consultation.

2

Patient sets up their medications with Elara AI

At home, the patient opens Carely and is guided by Elara through entering each medication, the dosage, and the schedule. Elara explains each step in plain language — no clinical literacy required. Setup takes under 5 minutes.

3

Carely reminds and checks in at every scheduled dose

Every day, at every dose time, the patient receives a gentle reminder and a friendly Elara check-in to confirm they've taken their medication. Missed doses are flagged. Family caregivers can receive automatic alerts when a dose is missed.

4

Better-adhered patients come in for follow-ups — not emergencies

Patients who take their medication consistently arrive at their next appointment with stable readings, not a deteriorating condition. Your next consultation is productive — not a crisis management session.

What Your Clinic Gets

Better outcomes. Less admin burden. Patients who show up prepared.

Carely doesn't replace your clinical judgment — it extends your reach between appointments. The patients who benefit most are your complex, high-medication-burden chronic disease patients.

📈

Better chronic disease outcomes between appointments

Consistent medication adherence improves HbA1c, blood pressure control, and lipid levels. Patients managed with Carely arrive at follow-ups with better readings — and your clinical interventions compound on a stable foundation.

📵

Fewer non-clinical calls to your reception

When family members know they'll be notified automatically if their parent misses a dose, they stop calling your clinic to check. Your reception team handles real appointment requests — not anxiety calls from worried families.

🤖

Elara AI provides daily wellness check-ins

Beyond reminders, Elara has a short friendly conversation with the patient each day — asking how they're feeling, noting anything unusual. This creates a light daily touchpoint between your clinical visits without consuming your clinical staff's time.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family caregivers are looped in automatically

For patients who consent, designated family members receive alerts when a dose is missed. This extends your care team to include the patient's support network — without requiring any extra clinical infrastructure.

📋

Adherence reports available for consenting patients

Patients who consent can share their Carely adherence history with your clinic. This gives you a real picture of what's actually happening with their medication — not just what they remember to tell you at the appointment.

No system integration — works on any smartphone

Carely requires no connection to your EMR, no IT project, and no change to your clinical workflow. It runs entirely on the patient's existing smartphone. Your staff need only a 30-second script to recommend it effectively.
