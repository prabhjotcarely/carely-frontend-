The Problem for Clinics

Your clinic sees thousands of patients. You see almost none of their daily data.

Between appointments, patients are on their own with chronic conditions. The gaps that form are invisible to your team — until they become emergencies.

📊

No population-level visibility

You manage hundreds of chronic-condition patients. Without real-time adherence data, you can’t identify which cohorts are at risk until they show up in the ED.

👤

Manual follow-up is impossible at scale

Your team can’t call every patient who misses a dose. You need intelligent automation that identifies risk and initiates follow-up without human overhead.

📋

Data trapped in per-appointment silos

EMR data reflects only what was captured at the appointment. The 99% of health behaviour happening at home is completely invisible to your clinical intelligence systems.

How It Works for Clinics

One integration. A whole new layer of care.

Carely runs silently in the background, handling the daily patient touchpoints your team never has time for.

1

Enroll your clinic in 15 minutes

We create a branded Carely onboarding flow for your practice. Reception gives patients a QR code or link — 30 seconds per patient, zero training required.

2

Elara checks in daily with every patient

Our AI caretaker handles daily medication reminders, mood check-ins, and symptom tracking across your entire enrolled population — automatically, 24/7.

3

Your dashboard fills with real data

You get a live population adherence dashboard, auto-triggered follow-up flags, and weekly summary reports per doctor, per department, and clinic-wide.
